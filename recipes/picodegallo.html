Pico De Gallo Salsa

Pico De Gallo de Alicia; this fresh dish will quench that spicy desire your guests will crave at your next Mexican themed barbecue. It's also good with your morning eggs, chips and salsa or whatever you want to add fresh spicy flavor to!

---How to make this spicy salsa---

Ingredients:

  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 red onion
  • 2 jalapeno peppers
  • 2 sprigs cilantro
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1/2 small lime - juiced
  • 1 tsp garlic salt - to taste

Directions:

  1. Dice tomatoes, finely chop onion and cilantro mix in bowl and set aside
  2. Press garlic, chop jalapeno peppers (keep jalapeno seeds for even more heat) - add to tomatoes, garlic and onion
  3. Add lime juice and garlic salt to mixture - stir
  4. Cover bowl and refrigerate at least 4 hours - overnight for even more flavor!
