Michelada

Michelada; a beer cocktail that will cool your tastebuds while enjoying some of the hottest Mexican foods!

---How to make this flavorful cocktail---

Ingredients:

  • coarse salt
  • 3 cubes ice
  • 1 lime - juiced
  • 1 can or bottle Mexican beer - 12 fluid ounce
  • 1/2 tsp steak sauce - sangrita is a good alternative
  • 1 dash cayenne pepper
  • 1 dash black pepper
  • 1 pinch dried oregano
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 pinch dried basil

Directions:

  1. Moisten the rim of a large beer glass, then press it into salt
  2. Place a few ice cubes in bottom of glass
  3. Pour in lime juice followed by half of the beer
  4. Season with; steak sauce, cayenne pepper, black pepper, oregano and basil
  5. Slowly pour in the remaining beer
  6. Stir and ENJOY!
